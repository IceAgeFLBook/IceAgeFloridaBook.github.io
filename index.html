About the Book

Each chapter contains a story of the animal from a day in their life, a discovery narrative about how the fossil was found that led to the story, a fact sheet with all the basic information about the animal, several color photographic plates of representative fossils of the animal, and a beautiful mural of the animal by famed paleo-artist Hermann Trappman.
